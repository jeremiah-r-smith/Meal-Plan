Chili

Description

This my personal Instapot chili recipe using the which never fails.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound of Ground Beef
  • 1 Can of Kidney or Chili Beans
  • 1 Can of Rotel
  • 1 Packet of Chili Mix
  • Little bit of olive oil

Recipe Steps

  1. Put olive oil into Instapot and set Instapot to saute.
  2. Put Ground Beef into Instapot and Brown until cooked.
  3. Put the rest of the ingredients into Instapot and mix.
  4. Set instapot to manual and 30 minutes and put lid on.
  5. Once the 30 minutes is up let Instapot depressurize on it's own for 5 minutes then manually release pressure.
  6. Serve with chili fixings
